No-Code vs Low-Code

No-code aims to allow users without any formal IT training to quickly setup websites and web applications for managing virtually any aspect of their business or project. The business users of FormulaDB no-code platform will achieve this by customizing one of the many ready-to use apps templates using simple constructs such as Tables, Spreadsheet-like Formulas, Pages.

Low-code aims to eliminate much of the repetitive and business-related work the developers and IT people usually have to perform. It frees valuable software development and IT specialist team members to focus on technical aspects that cannot be managed by business focused team members such as: integrations with custom and/or legacy systems, develop custom functions, develop custom Page components and widgets, troubleshoot issues with Pages, Tables, Formulas, performance, backups and restore, high availability and geographical redundancy, "big data".
